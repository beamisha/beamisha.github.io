About Me


Having completed my coursework at Queens University in Fall 2022, I have now applied to graduate with a Bachelor of Computing. My specialization in cognitive science has allowed me to take a series of courses in psychology and philosophy, in addition to the core computer science courses. I cherish the time I have spent learning from professors and peers, and helping others learn as a teaching assistant for several undergraduate courses.


In 2022, I began working with The CINDER Project (formerly BEAR), a student-led initiative focused on developing software that serves BIPOC businesses and communities. This work eventually led myself and Neila Toofuny to found a development agency called Codempowr (www.codempowr.com), which continues the work of The CINDER Project as part of it's core mission.


My long-term career ambitions are very much alligned with my interests and abilities. Over the course of my degree, I have acquired skills and knowledge in software development, artificial intelligence, and research practices. Through my work experience, I have also learned about various aspects of business development and sales. In the near future, I am focused on doing excellent development work with our team at Codempowr, and growing our portfolio.


If you'd like to get in touch, please connect with me on LinkedIn, or email me at alex@codempowr.com.
