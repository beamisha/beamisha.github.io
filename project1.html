Natural Language to PDDL


For my undergraduate thesis, I designed and tested novel techniques for the extraction of temporal PDDL code from natural language descriptions of action sequences. My work involved a literature review, the creation of a research paper and the development of a software program, all of which was performed under the supervision of Dr. Christian Muise. While completing this project, I was fortunate to be integrated into the MuLab, a graduate research group run by Dr. Muise.
